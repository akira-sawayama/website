Akira Sawayama, Managing Director

Akira Sawayama is the Managing Director of CELSUS Inc. He serves the executive recruitment needs of web, digital media, gaming, hi-tech, strategic consulting and PEF. He has conducted searches on behalf of clients ranging from venture-backed start-ups to large, multi-national companies.
Prior to founding CELSUS, he was Managing Partner of Boyden, a multi-national, retained executive search firm. Prior to executive search, he was Partner at Yahagi Consultants, a leading Japanese strategy consultancy where he advised enterprises in a range of industrial sectors including automotive, consumer electronics, food, pharmaceuticals, chemical, manufacturing, semiconductors, IT and telecommunications. As a Director, he formulated and implemented changes in organization, sourcing, procurement systems, process flows, cost structure, business mix and corporate culture.
Earlier he worked for Imperial Chemical Industries (ICI), a major British chemical manufacturer, in a variety of marketing and business development roles, including sales promotion. He also served as a technical/commercial liaison to leading Japanese automotive and paint firms on matter such as licensing deals and strategic alliances. He began his career with Xerox, US where he served as an analyst in the firm’s Information System Group with primary responsibility for market analysis.
He holds bachelor’s and master’s degrees in economics from Keio University, Tokyo.
